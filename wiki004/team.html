Our Team

Meet the passionate students and advisors behind the TimeEraser project

Experimental Group

Our experimental team consists of dedicated undergraduate researchers who designed and executed all the wet lab work for the TimeEraser project. They worked tirelessly to develop novel genetic circuits targeting cellular aging pathways, optimizing protocols and troubleshooting experiments to push the boundaries of synthetic biology.

Through countless hours in the lab, this team demonstrated remarkable perseverance and scientific rigor, contributing significantly to our project's success in the iGEM competition.

Wiki Team

The Wiki team worked diligently to document our project and create this interactive website. They transformed complex scientific concepts into accessible content while ensuring our work meets iGEM's rigorous documentation standards.

With skills ranging from web development to science communication, this team built a comprehensive digital presence that showcases our project to judges and the synthetic biology community worldwide.

Human Practices Team

Our Human Practices team engaged with stakeholders, experts, and the public to ensure our project addressed real-world needs responsibly. They organized outreach events, conducted interviews, and explored the ethical dimensions of our work.

By bridging the gap between science and society, this team helped shape TimeEraser into a project that considers not just scientific merit but also its potential impact on communities and individuals.
